❓ Help — Everything You Can Do

A full guide to Cosmic Explorer
🌌 Explore
  • Tap any planet, moon, dwarf planet, black hole, galaxy, or the Sun to fly there and open its encyclopedia
  • Tap "🔭 Explore" in the top-left to jump straight to any category — Galaxies, Black Holes, Stars, Exoplanets, Planets, Dwarf Planets, or Moons
  • Ask the search bar any space question for an instant answer
  • Tap "👣 Walk on..." on any page to imagine what it would feel like to stand there
  • Tap "📖 Flip Book" for a narrated, animated, read-aloud storybook explainer with highlighted words as it talks
  • Tap "📝 Quiz" on any page to test what you just learned
🧑‍🚀 Customize
  • Tap "☰ Menu" in the top-right to find all of these:
  • My Explorer — build your own astronaut avatar and set a username
  • My Age — set your age group (used to pick fun facts, and to safely match you with same-age friends)
  • Social — find and befriend fellow explorers, and start a 🎉 Party to explore together
  • Language — switch the app's menus and buttons into another language
  • Cosmic+ — an earned rank that unlocks the Galaxy Menu after 1 hour of exploring
  • Interstellar Explorer — a second earned rank that unlocks the Black Hole Menu after 30 minutes of exploring
  • Intermediate — a third earned rank that unlocks the Star Menu after 10 minutes of exploring
  • My Badges — view every rank badge you've earned so far, from quizzes and from exploring
🎛️ Controls
  • Pause / Play and the speed slider control how fast everything orbits
  • The Music button plays a calm space soundtrack you can turn on or off anytime
✨ Extras
  • Watch for a ☄️ falling comet coin every few minutes — tap it before it disappears for a real space history fact!
  • "← Back to the Solar System" in the top-left always brings you home from any page
